How we coach

Form-first, ego-free training.

You’ll never be asked to “just push through it”. We prioritise safe progressions, clear cues, and habit-building.

Small-group, coached sessions

Classes are capped at 12–14 people so your coach can watch your reps, adjust loads and help you move with confidence.

Progress tracking, not guesswork

We log your main lifts, show you week-on-week progress, and adjust your plan based on how your body responds.

Beginner-safe onboarding

New members go through a form check and movement screen so we know exactly how to scale your workouts.
