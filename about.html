About Fit&Fine

A gym built around coaching, not just machines.

Studio Fit&Fine was designed for people who want guidance, accountability and a program that adjusts as they grow stronger.

Coaching-first environment

Instead of leaving you alone with rows of equipment, our coaches run structured, small-group sessions with clear programming.

Science-based training

We combine progressive overload, movement quality and recovery protocols so you can push hard without burning out.

Community that shows up

You’ll train alongside people with similar goals, ages and stories — not influencers filming every set.

Questions

Membership FAQ.

Is there a lock-in period?

No long-term contracts. Plans renew monthly and you can change or cancel before your next billing date.

Can I pause my membership?

Travelling or injured? You can pause once every 6 months for up to 3 weeks with prior notice.

Do you offer corporate / group rates?

Yes – we have special packages for teams and companies. Reach out via the form below.
